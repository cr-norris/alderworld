  • Helping Vermonters outsmart winter and become more energy efficient.

  • Naming, Creative Direction & Branding

  • We like interactive design. So, we developed the idea of a customizable weatherization checklist that could offer new ideas for people looking to improve their home's performance. We spread the concept across the website, social media campaigns, and in-person with physical checklists available at hardware stores around the state for customers to take home.

  • At the core of the Button Up initiative is community. The culmination of the campaign was "Button Up Day," a single day of action where local hardware stores, community centers, and other organizations held events that encouraged the public to weatherize by providing information, demonstrations, rebates, and incentives.

  • Website & Content

  • The summit received attention on social media, blogs, and industry publications from the likes of the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change, the World Bank’s Connect4Climate initiative, 1% for the Planet, Green America, the American Sustainable Business Council, and over one hundred other organizations.

  • Twitter Activity

  • September 6–8, 2017

  • One of our objectives was to attract young entrepreneurs to the summit. We developed the #FutureFormula Pitch Contest that resulted in start-up companies from across the country submitting ideas to compete for seed funding.
